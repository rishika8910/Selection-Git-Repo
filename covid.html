Coronavirus (Covid-19)

Coronavirus is a major threat these days. As the vaccine to this virus is yet to be discovered, the main focus of the government and people is to prevent it from spreading.
So here are the main steps you can take to do your part in the prevention of spreading of this virus. Using this website you can also check what is the percentage of your input.

Precautions Your Score

Social Distancing

Stay at your homes and even if you have to go out for some emergency or necessity, stay at a safe distance of atleast 6 feet (about 2 arm's length) from other people. Avoid direct or indirect contact as long as possible

Wash Your Hands Frequently

It is very essential to wash your hands for atleast 20s every time after you touch any kind of foreign surface. It will prevent the virus which may have landed on your hand from such surfces from entering you.

Do not touch your face, nose or mouth

Prevent touching your face, nose or mouth as it may allow the virus to enter your body

Cover Your Cough

Cover your cough and sneeze preferably with your elbow and not with your hands as it will prevent the transmission of germs (or virus) from your hands to those of others.

In case of any symptom consult your doctor immediately
